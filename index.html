21M hard cap

Every four years the subsidy halves, capping supply and forcing the network to compete on fees.

Why it matters: Inflation resistance without a central bank.

Proof-of-Work security

Miners burn energy to anchor blocks. Attacking Bitcoin means competing with global industrial hardware.

Why it matters: Game theory makes fraud extremely expensive.

Keys, not accounts

Wallets prove ownership via private keys. Exchanges are convenience, not custody.

Why it matters: Self-custody removes single points of failure.

Layers stack

Lightning routes instant payments; the base chain settles final truth. Same asset, different speeds.

Why it matters: Scalability without sacrificing decentralization.
